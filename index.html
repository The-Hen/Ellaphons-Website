About Ellaphon

Ellaphon Taurariki-Legge is a soulful vocal artist and vocal coach based in Auckland, known for powerful stage presence, emotional storytelling, and a voice shaped by soul, jazz, and a cappella tradition.

His performances on The Voice New Zealand introduced audiences to a rich, expressive sound that feels both intimate and cinematic — equally at home in stripped-back vulnerability and commanding live performance.

As a coach, Ellaphon brings that same artistry into the lesson room, helping singers develop breath control, tone, confidence, phrasing, and presence. His approach is musical, supportive, and grounded in authentic expression rather than formula.
